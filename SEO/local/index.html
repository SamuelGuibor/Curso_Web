One-hour parking is typically available street-side, or use our valet service.

FROM THE EAST BAY - 80

  1. Take I80 W towards San Francisco
  2. Take the 5th St. exit
  3. Turn a sharp Left onto 5th St.
  4. Drive 3 blocks
  5. Turn Right onto Townsend
  6. Drive 2 blocks to 7th and Townsend

FROM THE SOUTH BAY - 101

  1. Take 101 North
  2. Take I80 E towards Bay Bridge
  3. Take the 7th St. exit (Get into the right lane)
  4. Merge onto Bryant St.
  5. Turn Right onto 5th St.
  6. Turn Right onto Townsend
  7. Drive 2 blocks to 7th and Townsend

FROM THE SOUTH BAY - 280

  1. Take 280 North
  2. Take the 6th St. exit (Get into the right lane)
  3. Follow 6th St. and get into the right lane
  4. Turn Right onto Bryant St.
  5. Turn Right onto 5th St.
  6. Turn Right onto Townsend
  7. Drive 2 blocks to 7th and Townsend

FROM THE NORTH BAY - 101

  1. Take 101 over the Golden Gate Bridge to the Lombard St. exit
  2. Follow Lombard to Van Ness St.
  3. Turn Right onto Van Ness
  4. Turn Left onto Eddy St.
  5. Follow this across Market, staying right, where it will turn into 5th St.
  6. Follow 5th all the way to Townsend, turn Right onto Townsend
  7. Drive 2 blocks to 7th and Townsend
